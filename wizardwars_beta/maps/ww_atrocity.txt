ww_atrocity - Capture the Grail
made by redux
-
Objective:  Enter the enemy castle and go to where their grail resides
Take it and return it to your battlements.
-
Scoring: You gain 10 points for every grail capture.  
Your team also gains 10 points for every capture
-
Other Notes: Grail carriers drop the grail when they die. 
Dropped grails return after 60 seconds.
