Volcano - Assassination / Escort
-
Objective: The Archmage must reach the volcano
alive. The Assassins need to kill The Archmage.
The Bodyguards need to protect The Archmage.
-
Scoring: If The Archmage dies, the Assassins get
25 points. If The Archmage reaches the volcano, 
he and the Bodyguards get 50 points.
-
Other Notes: Whenever The Archmage dies or
makes it to the volcano, all players are moved
back to their starting positions.
