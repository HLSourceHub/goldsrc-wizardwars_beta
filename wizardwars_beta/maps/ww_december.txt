ww_december - Capture and hold
made by redux
-
Objective:  Take your grail to the church and capture it at the altar.
Hold it as long as you can to gain points.
-
Scoring: You gain points every few seconds you hold the church.  
Your team gains additional points for capturing.
The player who captures recieves 5 additional frags.
-
Other Notes: Grail carriers drop the grail when they die. 
Dropped grails return after 30 seconds.
-
Map uses a heavily modified version of the koth entity set by FlintMan
