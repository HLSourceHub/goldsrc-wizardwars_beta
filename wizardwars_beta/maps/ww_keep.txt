================================
      THE WIZARD'S KEEP
    - Capture The Grail -
================================
by Rikers

Objective: Enter the enemy Base and make
your way to where their Grail resides. 
Grab it and bring it back to your Flag
Room. Place it on your grail to capture it.

Scoring: 10 Points to Capturing Person.
         10 Points to Capturing Team.

Other Notes: Grail carriers drop the Flag when
they die. Dropped Grails return to their Base
after 60 seconds.
