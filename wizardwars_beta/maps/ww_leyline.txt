ww_leyline - Sequential Territorial Control
-------------------------------------------
A DuckHunt Creation
-------------------------------------------

Objective:  Capture each node in order of
their distance from your spawn to open a
portal out of the desolate wasteland.

As each node is captured, a portal opens
at points further from your spawn.

When the final node is captured, a portal
nearby opens to escape through. This
portal remains open for 30 seconds.
-
Scoring:
Node Capture: 1 personal point, 1 team point
Final Node: 5 personal points, 5 team points
Escape: 1 personal point, 1 team point
-
Other Notes: Standing in the beam of a node
your team controls will restore health, mana
and sheidls.
